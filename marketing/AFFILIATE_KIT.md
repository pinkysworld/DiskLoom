DiskLoom is a modern, read-only drive-health and backup-readiness monitor for macOS. It combines Apple NVMe health, supported SMART diagnostics, one-year history, practical replacement guidance, and Time Machine readiness without turning missing USB telemetry into a false healthy result.

## Required compatibility wording

External SMART support depends on macOS and the USB or Thunderbolt bridge exposing command passthrough. Some enclosures work on Windows but not on macOS. DiskLoom reports those cases as unknown rather than healthy.

Do not claim that DiskLoom can predict an exact failure date, repair a drive, recover files, bypass blocked USB bridges, or guarantee that a healthy drive will not fail.
